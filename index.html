AsokoInsight

AsokoInsight is a startup that provides information about companies in South Africa.

They wanted to redesign their site in order to make their core functionality more accessible and easier to use.

I focused on their main usecase, and in the end we had a really simple way of finding information about South Africa companies, without concentration on insignificant stuff for business. I also participated in programming interface with html/css/less.

Lifereaders

Lifereaders is a platform where it is possible to have a consultation about your life choices.

They wanted to update their service in order to look fresh and modern, but with interface preservation in order not to lose their old customers.

I increased the significant stuff in a new design, we had images 4 times bigger than previously. I made design lighter visually and fixed the usecases that were too complicated. I also contributed to programming interface with html/css/scss.

SmartexLab

SmartexLab is an outsource company located in Belarus.

Their priorities were to introduce their specialists, completed projects and the speciality that SmartexLab has.

I took a lot of pictures of their office and people working there. I also did screenshots of projects they had done. In the design there are no stock images, only the real ones that show working processes. Moreover I took part in programming interface with html/css/scss.

MediaVillage

MediaVillage is a trade marketing, communications, insights and business connections firm founded by Jack Myers.

They wanted to update the look of their articles in order to make them more captivating and appealing.

I made a couple of concepts of an article page and a ’list of articles‘ page. I tried to make all the blocks on the pages diverse in order to avoid monotony of the same structure of content, but still generic to make it easy to upload new information.
