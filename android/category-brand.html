    Tommy Bahama Furniture

    The furniture brand of Tommy Bahama is renowned for its refined interpretation of exceptional island living. The company believes that enjoying the casual comfort of the island life should be easy for peace of mind and security. The home furniture collection of unique furnishings evokes a sense of intrigue and romance through the combination of exotic natural materials, rich finishes and eclectic design. You can enjoy relaxing, long weekends and capture the essence of living on a resort with the casual and idyllic style of the brand. It is a worldwide manufacturer and marketer of unique home furnishings, and a forerunner in the industry in lifestyle marketing and innovative design. Its award-winning product assortment of living room furniture and upholstered furniture covers a vast range of styles and designs. Residential products are provided through interior designers, to-the-trade showrooms, design firms and independent retailers. The company also produces custom contract and contract furnishings for commercial and hospitality use. The company started in August 1992, with corporate headquarters in Seattle.

    The brand is known for its high-end mens and womens clothing, shoes and accessories, and of course, a complete home furnishings collection. In fact, there are more than 130 stores across the globe, with a restaurant and bar in 14 of those locations. These products are available at the finest retailers in the nation, which entails Nordstrom and various specialty retailers. Your aspirations and way of life for your living space are distinctively personal. The passion of the company is based on assisting you in a home dcor that brings your vision to life, with remarkable styling for every lifestyle and room. Whether your taste is traditional or contemporary, formal or casual, the brand has designs to inspire you. With an aesthetic that is chic yet restrained, Pan-Asian design conveys terrific value to aesthetic composition, sleek disciplined lines and the objective of instilling tranquility into modern contemporary dcor. The result is a stunning contemporary expression of personal style, with an ambiance which feels cozy and relaxed. Statement pieces of Tommy Bahama define the home dcor, while custom upholstery provides an invitation to enjoy the relaxed comfort of cool elegance.
