Product & UX Strategy

We will work with you to ensure business objectives are in alignment to end-user needs and to ensure the integrity of the solution throughout the decision-making process. We know the user-experience rules, but more importantly we know when to break them in order to deliver maximum value. Throughout any engagement, Contexxt works closely with its clients to construct a set of project goals that are directly aligned with overall corporate objectives.

Services include

  1. User Research: diary studies, interviews, surveys, usability testing
  2. Market Research: Competitive analysis, defining and prioritising target market…
  3. Product Strategy Workshop: business requirements, high level user experience
  4. Personas & Scenarios
  5. Requirements gathering and scoping: stakeholder interviews, technical analysis
  1. Information architecture: card sorting, affinity diagrams
  2. User journeys and user flows
  3. Wireframes
  4. UI/Visual Design
  5. Heuristic Evaluation
  6. Prototyping
  7. Product Roadmaps

Rapid POCs

As you can see in Labs, we love to experiment with new technology and are always looking for new applications for existing capabilities. We can work with you to quickly repurpose any technology to suit your vision and help you test out new features and functionality. We believe POCs should be quick and economical, hence the term “Rapid POCs”.

Rapid POCs can help you

  1. See if something is technologically feasible within your budget
  2. Illustrate to stakeholders your vision for the future
  3. Kick-start a proposition by bringing your idea to life
  4. Test out your idea in the marketplace
